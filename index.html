What is BarCamp Manchester

BarCamp Manchester is a technology un-conference held annually in Manchester, NH. Like a traditional conference, we have a venue with session rooms, meeting areas, and food for our attendees. However, at an unconference, the attendees provide the content.

Developers, designers, hackers, and UX pros all present on the topics of their choosing. These can include tutorials, demos of new technology, panels discussion issues facing our industry, or something a little less typical. The event is 100% free, we only ask that everybody participate in some way.

BarCamp Manchester generally focuses on web development and software, but we also have a handful of hardware hackers, entrepanuers, game developers, and other assorted tech topics.
